Protect The World Around You With Humanely

Know what's happening in your surrounding

The Powerful Free Safety App Ever Created. Creating a safe neigborhood by the people, for the people.

“We believe in giving people a way to use their phones and internet as a power to protect a neighbor, to prevent a tragedy, and to count on one another and to create a safer locality for each other, with each other.”

Get Safety Alerts When They Matter To You- Real Time

Be situationally aware. Humanely alerts go out within seconds or user report. If there’s an incident, like a robbery, nearby, you’ll know to avoid that area.

Know what’s happening around so you and your neighbors can stay safe.

Connect with your family, friends, and neighbors on Humanely and find out when something important is happening around them.

When you can, contribute to help resolve a situation.

Now you can use Humanely to broadcast and share relevant updates with others. You can also leave comments, upvotes which will help locate missing people and share information about evacuation centers during natural disasters.
